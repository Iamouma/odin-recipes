Classic Caesar Salad

Description

A refreshing and crunchy Caesar Salad featuring crisp romaine lettuce, creamy homemade dressing, golden croutons, and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

This timeless salad pairs perfectly with grilled chicken or pasta and makes a healthy, delicious meal any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ¬Ω cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ¬Ω cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Optional: grilled chicken or bacon bits

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce, then pat dry.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  3. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese; mix gently.
  4. Top with extra cheese, a dash of black pepper, and serve immediately.
