Caesar Salad

Description

A fresh Caesar salad made with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, crunchy croutons, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce and place in a large bowl.
  2. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss gently.
  4. Top with extra cheese and serve immediately.
