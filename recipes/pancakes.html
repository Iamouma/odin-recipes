Pancakes

Description

Fluffy homemade pancakes that are perfect for breakfast or brunch. Theyâ€™re quick, delicious, and can be served with syrup, fruits, or butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in another.
  2. Combine both and stir until smooth.
  3. Pour batter onto a greased pan and cook each side until golden.
