Classic Homemade Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a hearty Italian favorite made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella. Each bite is a perfect blend of comfort and flavor ‚Äî ideal for Sunday dinners or family gatherings.

It‚Äôs simple to prepare, satisfying, and can even be made ahead of time for busy weeknights. Serve with garlic bread and a fresh salad for the ultimate meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef, then stir in the marinara sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer noodles, ricotta, sauce, and mozzarella.
  5. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  6. Bake uncovered for 40‚Äì45 minutes or until golden and bubbling.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes, garnish with basil, and serve warm.
