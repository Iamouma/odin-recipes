Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a hearty Italian dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

It’s easy to make, filling, and can be prepared ahead of time for convenience.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente and drain.
  2. Cook ground beef in a skillet and mix with marinara sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
